# Business Insights

## Revenue Analysis

- Revenue contribution was analyzed across genders and customer groups.
- Category-level analysis identified major revenue-driving segments.

## Customer Segmentation

- Customers were segmented into New, Returning, and Loyal groups based on purchase history.
- Loyal customers demonstrated stronger purchasing behavior.

## Subscription Analysis

- Subscriber and non-subscriber spending patterns were compared.
- Subscription trends revealed opportunities for customer retention strategies.

## Product Analysis

- Top-rated products were identified using customer review ratings.
- Discount-dependent products were analyzed to understand purchasing behavior.

## Demographic Insights

- Revenue and sales trends were compared across age groups.
- Purchasing behavior varied significantly across demographic segments.

## Recommendations

- Focus marketing efforts on high-value customer segments.
- Improve subscription conversion strategies.
- Use demographic insights for targeted promotions.
